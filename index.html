My favorite game is

VALORANT

VALORANT is a character-based 5v5 tactical shooter set on the global stage. You have to work with your team and use your tactical abilities to outshine your competition.

Why Gaming is good for you

Meet new people around the world

Gaming lets you converse with people from anywhere in the world, whether that's a friend or someone you're playing against. Despite criticism that gaming isolates players, it's the exact opposite.

Exercise your strategy skills

Gaming takes skill, coordination, and strategy to succeed. You're always using your brain to develop ways to outsmart and outwit friends.

Blow off some steam

We can talk about game strategy or knowledge all day long, but at the end of the day? Gaming with friends lets you relax after a hard day's work, and it's a great defense mechanism against stress.
